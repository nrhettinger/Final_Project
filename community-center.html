Oldham County Parks & Recreation Department offers the John W. Black Community Center at affordable prices for your event. Elegant, clean, informal or formal, our center offers space for up to 320 people. A perfect backdrop for a wedding reception, business meeting, family reunion or party with plenty of parking.

Our Community Center Services Include:

  • Event Venue - Affordable yet elegant for your wedding reception, meeting, lecture or party
  • Variety - Can be accommodated for groups from 60 - 320 people with or without tables
  • Backdrop - A serene woodsy setting without being rustic - simple yet elegant

Rental Rates

  • Rental time includes set-up and clean-up time.
  • Events must be finished by 10 pm with all clean-up completed by 11pm.
  • Kitchen fee is $25.00.
